04Bring your own key

Your key. Your provider. Your bill.

Ownkey doesn't run servers between you and your AI. There's no middleman to trust, because there's no middleman.

step 01

Get a key

From OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, OpenRouter, or your own endpoint.

step 02

Paste it once

It's stored on your device and nowhere else. Swap providers whenever you like.

step 03

Speak

Audio goes from your device to your provider, and text comes back. That's the whole route.

No account. No subscription. No telemetry. Pay your provider, not us.
